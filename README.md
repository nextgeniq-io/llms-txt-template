# llms-txt-template

A starter `llms.txt` file for brands that want to control how AI engines summarize and crawl their site.

## What is llms.txt?

`llms.txt` is a proposed standard (analogous to `robots.txt`) that tells AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude how to interpret your site's content. Most brands haven't set one up yet. Without it, engines crawl and summarize without your input.

## Quick start

1. Copy `llms.txt` into the root of your site (`https://yoursite.com/llms.txt`).
2. Edit the sections to describe your brand, key pages, and what you do not want AI engines to surface.
3. Validate at [NextGenIQ Crawlability Checker](https://nextgeniq.io/diagnostics/crawlability-checker).
